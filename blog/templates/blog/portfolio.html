PicoCTF 2024

PicoCTF is a renowned online cybersecurity competition designed for middle and high school students, created by the cybersecurity experts at Carnegie Mellon University (CMU). This capture-the-flag (CTF) competition challenges participants to solve a series of computer security problems across various categories such as cryptography, binary exploitation, reverse engineering, and more. My team (Buffer)&Overbyte placed 12th (tied with 9th) of 1,329 teams nationally in the High School picoCTF 2024 competition. A writeup for the competition can be accessed through the image.
